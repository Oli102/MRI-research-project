Importance of MRI Technology

  • Non-invasive and accurate: Provides a highly accurate way to diagnose conditions without cutting the body.
  • Crucial for screening: Safely detects cancers, tumors, and internal injuries, preventing unnecessary and risky exploratory surgery.
  • Zero radiation risk: Uses magnets and radio waves instead of harmful ionizing radiation (like X-rays), making it extremely safe for patients.
  • "Faith that does justice is a faith that does science": Using technology for medicine serves the greater good and fulfills our calling to be stewards of creation. By caring for the sick and vulnerable and preserving life and health, we put faith into action.
